FAQ

Q) What happens in the event??

Startup Expo 2024 is a major event that typically features a variety of activities aimed at fostering entrepreneurship, leadership and networking among startups, investors, and industry professionals.

Q) When is the Event for this year??

The Startup Expo 2024 event is scheduled to take place from August 20th to August 24th. .

Q) What do the attendees get??

Attendees of Startup Expo 2024 gain access to valuable networking opportunities, insights from industry experts, exposure to innovative products and services, potential investment opportunities, and the chance to participate in workshops, panel discussions, and pitch sessions. .

Q) Is there any Registration Fees??

No, there is no registration fee for attending Startup Expo 2024.
